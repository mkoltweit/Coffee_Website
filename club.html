Steamers Coffee is more than a coffee shop...

Steamers Coffee is a place for community. Every Friday and Saturday night is an opportunity to showcase your unique skills and talents.


Our goal is to provide an outlet within our community to come together and share in talent and conversation. We encourage all levels of performers, from novice to expert, to share their talent in front of a live audience.



Coffee Club Guidelines

  • Performances begin at 7pm. Closing time is 10:00pm.
  • Time slots are available through our sign up form
  • Time slots are 20 minutes total
  • When you are next, please have equipment ready to take the stage.
  • Be considerate of other performers, venue, and equipment
  • This is a family event. Please keep songs and spoken word free of profanity or vulgar material.
